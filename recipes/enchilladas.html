Enchilladas

My dogs famous enchiladas

Let me tell you a story about my dog. He was born blind and had a bad life. It wasn't until later in life when he finally found his true potential. He started making enchilladas and started a new adventure in life. He is now the king of Enchilllads.

ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 cup chunky salsa
  • 6 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 3 cups shredded, cooked chicken
  • 8 inch flour tortillas
  • 1 can enchilada sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mexican cheese blend

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Line a drimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil, then spray with cooking spray.
  2. Combine salsa and cream cheese in a large pan over medium heat; cook and stir until cream cheese has melted and mixture is creamy and throroughly combined, 2-5 minutes. Add chicken and cook until heated through, 2-3 minutes
  3. Spook chicken mixture into tortillas and roll into enchiladas. Place, seam-side down, on the prepared baking sheet. Top with enchilada sauce.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven in 10 minutes. Top with Mexican cheese. Bake until cheese is melted, 5 to 10 minutes more. Serve hot.
